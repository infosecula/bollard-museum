Privacy Statement

At The Bollard Museum, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle and store your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information we collect: We may collect your email address for communication purposes. We also use Countly Flex for analytics, which may collect non-personally identifiable information about your use of our website.

2. How we use your information: We use your email address to send updates, newsletters, and respond to inquiries. Countly Flex helps us understand user behavior and improve our website.

3. Data storage and security: We store your data securely and have measures in place to prevent unauthorized access. We will not share your information with third parties except as required by law.

4. Your rights: Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data. You also have the right to data portability.

5. Contact us: If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please contact us at info@bollardmuseum.org.uk.

This policy is subject to change. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. Please review this policy regularly for updates.
